How It Was Developed

Hertzion is a low-frequency vibroacoustic device, originally based on foundational technology from a 2012 study, and has since been refined to meet modern wellness needs. Developed around the scientific potential of 40Hz vibration stimulation to promote neuronal differentiation and aid in the recovery of damaged neural tissue, Hertzion uses non-invasive brain stimulation technology. It is designed for everyday use to support cognitive health and emotional well-being.
